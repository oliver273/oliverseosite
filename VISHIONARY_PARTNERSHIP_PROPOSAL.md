# Partnership Proposal: Vishionary & OliverSEO

## The Opportunity

**Problem:** Vishionary receives frequent requests from clients for website design services, but doesn't currently offer this service.

**Solution:** Partner with OliverSEO to handle all website requests seamlessly, providing value to Vishionary's clients while generating additional revenue.

---

## Partnership Model Options

### Option 1: Referral Partnership (Recommended)
- **How it works:** Vishionary refers clients who need websites to OliverSEO
- **Vishionary benefits:**
  - Provide complete service to clients (one-stop shop)
  - Generate referral commission (15-20% of project value)
  - No additional work or overhead
  - Enhanced client satisfaction
- **Client benefits:**
  - Trusted referral from Vishionary
  - Professional website services
  - Seamless experience

### Option 2: White-Label Partnership
- **How it works:** OliverSEO builds websites "powered by Vishionary"
- **Vishionary benefits:**
  - Can offer websites as their own service
  - Set their own pricing/markup
  - Full brand control
- **OliverSEO benefits:**
  - Consistent work pipeline
  - Brand exposure through Vishionary

### Option 3: Hybrid Model
- **How it works:** Combination of referral and white-label based on client needs
- **Flexibility:** Choose the best approach per project
