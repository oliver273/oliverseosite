## ðŸ“‹ STEP-BY-STEP SETUP

### STEP 1: Create Square Account

1. Go to: **https://squareup.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"** (top right)
3. Choose **"Invoicing"** or **"Point of Sale"**
4. Enter your:
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Business name
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. Verify your email (check inbox)

**Time:** 5 minutes

---

### STEP 2: Set Up Business Profile

1. Log into Square Dashboard
2. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Business Information**
3. Fill in:
   - **Business Name:** Your business name
   - **Business Address:** Your address
   - **Phone Number:** Your phone
   - **Website:** Your website (if you have one)
   - **Tax ID:** Your EIN/SSN (if applicable)
4. Click **"Save"**

**Optional:**
- Upload your logo (Settings â†’ Branding)
- Set brand colors
- Add custom footer message

**Time:** 10 minutes

---

### STEP 3: Connect Bank Account

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Bank Accounts**
2. Click **"Add Bank Account"**
3. Enter:
   - **Routing Number:** (9 digits, from your check)
   - **Account Number:** (from your check)
   - **Account Type:** Checking or Savings
4. Click **"Add Bank Account"**
5. Square will make 2 small deposits (takes 1-2 business days)
6. Verify amounts in Square dashboard

**Time:** 5 minutes (plus 1-2 days for verification)

---

### STEP 4: Set Up Payment Processing

**Credit Card Processing:**
- Square automatically enables this
- No additional setup needed
- Fees: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards
- ACH (bank transfer) - lower fees

**Time:** Automatic (already enabled)

---

### STEP 5: Create Your First Invoice

1. Go to **Invoices** â†’ **Create Invoice**
2. Fill in customer info:
   - **Name:** Client's name
   - **Email:** Client's email
   - **Address:** (optional)
   - **Phone:** (optional)
3. Add invoice details:
   - **Invoice Number:** Auto-generated (or custom)
   - **Date:** Today's date
   - **Due Date:** (e.g., 7 days from now)
4. Add line items:
   - **Description:** "Website Design - Professional Package"
   - **Quantity:** 1
   - **Price:** $499
   - **Tax:** Add if applicable
5. Add notes (optional):
   - Payment terms
   - What's included
   - Delivery timeline
6. Click **"Save"**

**Time:** 5 minutes

---

### STEP 6: Customize Invoice Template

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Invoice Settings**
2. Customize:
   - **Logo:** Upload your logo
   - **Colors:** Match your brand
   - **Terms & Conditions:** Add your terms
   - **Footer Message:** Custom message
   - **Payment Methods:** Which to accept
3. Click **"Save"**

**Time:** 10 minutes

---

### STEP 7: Send Invoice

1. Open your invoice
2. Click **"Send Invoice"**
3. Choose method:
   - **Email** (recommended)
   - **Text Message**
   - **Link** (copy and send yourself)
4. Client receives:
   - Professional invoice PDF
   - "Pay Now" button
   - Multiple payment options

**Time:** 2 minutes

---

### STEP 8: Track Payments

1. Go to **Invoices** â†’ **View All**
2. See status:
   - **Sent** (waiting for payment)
   - **Paid** (green checkmark)
   - **Overdue** (red)
3. Set up notifications:
   - **Email when paid:** Settings â†’ Notifications
   - **Reminder for overdue:** Settings â†’ Invoice Settings

**Time:** 2 minutes to set up
