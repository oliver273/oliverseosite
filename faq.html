Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about website design services, pricing, timelines, and more.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on the package you choose:

  • • Quick Win Website Upgrade: 48-72 hours
  • • Essential Package: 5-7 business days
  • • Professional Package: 7-10 business days
  • • Premium Package: 10-14 business days

Do you provide hosting?

Yes! Essential, Professional, and Premium packages include free hosting for the first year. This includes:

  • ✓ Fast, reliable hosting
  • ✓ Free SSL certificate
  • ✓ CDN for fast loading worldwide
  • ✓ No setup fees

Note: The Quick Win Website Upgrade package does not include hosting, as it's designed for quick improvements to existing websites.

After the first year, hosting is available for $35/month. You can also choose to host elsewhere if you prefer.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. Your site will look great and function perfectly on any screen size, from smartphones to large desktop monitors.

Can I make changes after the website is live?

Yes! Each package includes revision rounds:

  • • Quick Win: 1-2 revisions
  • • Essential: 2 rounds of revisions
  • • Professional: 4 rounds of revisions
  • • Premium: 6 rounds of revisions

After the included revisions, I offer ongoing maintenance and updates at reasonable rates.

Do you work with businesses outside Wyoming?

Yes! While I specialize in Wyoming businesses and have a deep understanding of the local market, I work with clients nationwide. I'm available for calls and can work remotely with businesses anywhere in the United States.

What's included in the Quick Win Website Upgrade?

The Quick Win package is perfect for businesses that need results fast. It includes:

  • ✓ Homepage redesign or new one-page site
  • ✓ Mobile optimization
  • ✓ Clear call-to-action (call / form / booking)
  • ✓ Speed & layout improvements
  • ✓ Basic SEO + tracking setup
  • ✓ 48-72 hour turnaround
  • ✓ 1-2 revisions

Pricing: $250 – $400 OR $50/hr

What's the difference between the packages?

Essential ($1,500-$2,500): Perfect for small businesses getting started. One-page responsive website with basic SEO. Turn visitors into leads with a clean, conversion-focused site.

Professional ($2,500-$4,000): Ideal for businesses ready to grow and compete locally. Multi-page website (up to 5 pages) with custom design, enhanced SEO, and interactive elements. Built for Trust, Credibility, and Lead Flow.

Premium ($5,000-$7,000): For established businesses ready to dominate their market online. A complete digital presence that builds authority and drives consistent results. Multi-page website (up to 10 pages) with fully custom design, premium SEO, performance optimization, advanced features, and free hosting for 1 year.

Do you handle SEO optimization?

Yes! All packages include SEO optimization:

  • ✓ Essential: Basic SEO optimization
  • ✓ Professional: Enhanced SEO optimization
  • ✓ Premium: Premium SEO optimization

This includes proper meta tags, structured data, mobile optimization, fast loading speeds, and SEO-friendly code structure to help your site rank in search engines.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy:

  1. 1. Fill out the contact form on the homepage
  2. 2. I'll get back to you as soon as possible (usually within 24 hours)
  3. 3. We'll discuss your project and I'll provide a custom quote
  4. 4. Once approved, I'll start building your website
  5. 5. You'll receive regular updates and can request revisions
  6. 6. Your website goes live!

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, checks, and Square Up for online invoices. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront to begin work, and 50% upon completion. For Quick Win packages, payment can be structured based on the hourly rate or fixed price agreement.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! I can redesign your existing website or build a completely new one. The Quick Win Website Upgrade is specifically designed for businesses that need a fast improvement to their current site. I can work with your existing content or help create new content for your site.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! After your website launches, I offer ongoing support and maintenance services at $50/hour. This can include content updates, feature additions, security updates, and technical support. We can discuss a maintenance plan that works for your business needs.
