## üé¨ REMOVE LOADING SCREENS (2-3 Minutes)

### **Method 1: Manual (Fastest)**

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Import Video:**
   - Bottom left: Click "Media" tab
   - Right-click in media pool ‚Üí "Import Media"
   - Select your video file

2. **Add to Timeline:**
   - Drag video from media pool to timeline (bottom)

3. **Find Loading Screens:**
   - Press `Space` to play video
   - Watch for loading screens

4. **Remove Loading Screen:**
   - When you see loading screen start:
     - Press `I` (marks start)
   - When loading screen ends:
     - Press `O` (marks end)
   - Press `Delete` (removes it)

5. **Repeat:**
   - Continue through video
   - Remove all loading screens
   - Takes 2-3 minutes for 10-minute video

6. **Export:**
   - Click "Deliver" tab (top)
   - Format: MP4
   - Click "Add to Render Queue"
   - Click "Render All"
   - Done! (No loading screens, instant export)

---

### **Method 2: Auto-Detect (AI-Powered)**

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Import Video:**
   - Same as Method 1

2. **Auto-Detect Scenes:**
   - Right-click timeline ‚Üí "Detect Scene Cuts"
   - AI finds all scene changes automatically
   - Loading screens usually between scenes

3. **Review Cuts:**
   - Play through detected cuts
   - Identify loading screen segments

4. **Delete Loading Screens:**
   - Select loading screen segments
   - Press `Delete`

5. **Export:**
   - Same as Method 1

**Time:** 1-2 minutes (even faster!)
