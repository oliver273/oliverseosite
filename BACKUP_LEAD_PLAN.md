## TIER 2: Cold Outreach (High Volume)

### 8. Cold Email Local Businesses
**Find businesses without websites:**
1. Google: "[city] [business type]" (e.g., "Casper plumber")
2. Look for businesses with bad/no websites
3. Find their email on Google/Yelp/Facebook

**Email template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about [Business Name]'s website

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website (or has one that could use an update).

I help local businesses get more customers with professional websites.

Would you be open to a quick chat about getting you more calls?

I can have a site live in 7 days.

Portfolio: [link]

Thanks,
Oliver
```

**Volume:** Send 20-50 emails/day

### 9. Walk-In (Local)
If you're local in Wyoming:
- Visit local businesses
- Ask to speak to owner
- Hand them a card/flyer
- Offer to help with website

## What to Record (OBS Setup)

### Video Ideas:

#### 1. "Watch Me Build a Website in 2 Hours"
- Record full screen
- Speed up to 10-15 minutes
- Add voiceover explaining what you're doing
- Show the final result

#### 2. "I Built a $2,000 Website for FREE (Tutorial)"
- Build a sample site for a fake business
- Explain your process
- Show before/after

#### 3. "5 Website Mistakes Killing Your Business"
- Screen record bad websites
- Explain what's wrong
- Show how you'd fix it

#### 4. "How I Built [Client Name]'s Website"
- Case study video
- Show the process
- Show results (if possible)

#### 5. "Website Review: I'll Fix Your Site Live"
- Ask for submissions
- Review and critique websites
- Offer to fix them

#### 6. Timelapse: "Building a Website in 60 Seconds"
- Record full build
- Speed up to 60 seconds
- Satisfying to watch

---

## OBS Recording Setup

### Settings:
1. **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p)
2. **FPS:** 30
3. **Format:** MP4
4. **Audio:** Record your microphone + system audio

### What to Record:
- Your screen (Cursor, browser, code editor)
- Your face (optional, but builds trust)
- Your voice (explain what you're doing)

### Tips:
- Clean your desktop before recording
- Use a nice dark theme in your editor
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Edit out boring parts

## TIER 4: Partnerships & Referrals

### 10. Partner with Other Freelancers
**Find:**
- Copywriters (they need websites for clients)
- Graphic designers (they need devs)
- Marketing agencies (they outsource)
- SEO specialists (they need sites to optimize)

**Reach out:**
```
Hey [Name],

I'm a web designer and I noticed you do [their service].

Do your clients ever need websites? I'd love to partner up - you refer website projects to me, I refer [their service] projects to you.

Let me know if you're interested!

Oliver
```

### 11. Referral Program
**Offer existing clients:**
- $100-$200 for every referral that becomes a client
- Or 10% of project value
- Makes them actively look for referrals
