## Phase 1: Immediate Actions (Do Today)

### 1. Submit to Google Search Console
**Time:** 15 minutes

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Start now" and sign in with Google
3. Add your property: `https://oliverseo.com`
4. Verify ownership (use HTML tag method - easiest)
5. Submit your sitemap: `https://oliverseo.com/sitemap.xml`

**Why:** This tells Google your site exists and helps it get indexed faster.

### 2. Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools
**Time:** 10 minutes

1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Import from Google Search Console (easiest)
4. Or add site manually
5. Submit sitemap

**Why:** Bing powers Yahoo and DuckDuckGo searches too.

### 3. Create/Claim Google Business Profile
**Time:** 30 minutes

1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Search for "OliverSEO"
3. If not found, click "Add your business"
4. Fill in:
   - Business name: OliverSEO
   - Category: Web Designer (primary), Web Development Company
   - Service area: Wyoming (select all cities you serve)
   - Contact: oliver@oliverseo.com
   - Website: https://oliverseo.com
   - Description: "Professional website design for small businesses. Get a website that brings you 3-5x more calls and inquiries. Fast 7-14 day delivery. 100% money-back guarantee. Serving Wyoming businesses including Casper, Cheyenne, Laramie, and Gillette."
5. Add services:
   - Essential Website Package - $1,500-$2,500
   - Professional Website Package - $2,500-$4,000
   - Premium Website Package - $4,000-$7,000
6. Add photos (screenshots of portfolio)
7. Verify (postcard or phone)

**Why:** Google Business Profile is the #1 local SEO factor. You WILL show up in "web design near me" searches.

Oliver

#### 2. HARO (Help a Reporter Out)
- Sign up: https://www.helpareporter.com
- Get daily emails from journalists needing sources
- Respond to web design / small business queries
- Get quoted in articles with backlinks

#### 3. Testimonials
- Give testimonials to tools you use
- They often link back to your site
- Examples: hosting companies, design tools, business software

#### 4. Local News
- Pitch stories to local Wyoming news:

## Tracking Progress

### Set Up Tracking:

1. **Google Search Console**
   - Track impressions, clicks, position
   - See which keywords you're ranking for
   - Monitor indexing status

2. **Google Analytics** (Already set up)
   - Track website traffic
   - See where visitors come from
   - Monitor conversions (form submissions)

3. **Rank Tracker** (Optional)
   - Use free tool: https://www.seobility.net/en/rankingcheck/
   - Or: https://whatsmyserp.com
   - Track your keyword rankings weekly

### Key Metrics to Watch:
- **Impressions:** How often you appear in search
- **Clicks:** How many people click through
- **Position:** Your average ranking position
- **Conversions:** Form submissions from organic traffic
