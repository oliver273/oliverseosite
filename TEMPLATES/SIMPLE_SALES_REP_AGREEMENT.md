### AGREEMENT TERMS

**1. Commission Structure:**
- First client closed: 40-50% of total package price
- Ongoing clients: 25-30% of total package price
- Commission is paid ONLY when client completes full payment (both deposit and final payment)
- Commission will be paid within 7 days of receiving client's final payment
- Commission is NOT paid on deposits alone

**2. Payment Process:**
- Client pays 50% deposit → Company starts building
- Client pays final 50% → Company pays commission within 7 days
- If client cancels after deposit: No commission owed

**3. What Sales Rep Does:**
- Find businesses needing websites
- Reach out via email, social media, etc.
- Close sales
- Direct leads to oliverseo.com
- Send client info to Company when ready to pay

**4. What Company Provides:**
- All sales materials (package details, email templates, objection responses)
- Website link (oliverseo.com)
- Support and answers to questions
- Commission payment when client completes payment

**5. Termination:**
- Either party can end this agreement at any time
- Commission is only paid for completed sales before termination

**6. Upgrade to Formal Contract:**
- This is a simple agreement
- Will be upgraded to formal contract when Company turns 18 (on [your birthday])
- Terms will remain the same, just more formal structure
