# Independent Contractor Sales Agreement

**This is a template. Customize with your name/business name and have both parties sign.**

---

## INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR SALES AGREEMENT

**Date:** ___________

**Parties:**
- **Company:** [Your Name/Business Name] ("Company")
- **Contractor:** [Sales Rep Name] ("Contractor")

---

### 1. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

**Commission Rates:**
- First client closed: 40-50% of total package price
- Ongoing clients: 25-30% of total package price

**Commission is paid ONLY when client completes full payment (both deposit and final payment).**

**Commission will be paid within 7 days of receiving client's final payment.**

**Commission is NOT paid on deposits alone.**

---

### 2. PAYMENT PROCESS

**Payment Process:**
1. Client pays 50% deposit → Company starts building
2. Client pays final 50% → Company pays commission within 7 days
3. If client cancels after deposit: No commission owed

---

### 3. CANCELLATION POLICY

**If client cancels after deposit but before final payment:**
- No commission is owed
- Deposit is retained by Company to cover work completed

---

### 4. REFUND POLICY

**If client requests refund within 30 days of final payment:**
- Commission will be clawed back (returned to Company)
- Contractor must return commission within 14 days of refund request

---

### 5. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee.

Contractor is responsible for their own taxes.

Company does not provide benefits, insurance, or equipment.

---

### 6. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with written notice.

Commission is only paid for completed sales before termination.

---

### 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Contractor agrees not to share client information, pricing, or business strategies with competitors.

---

### 8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes will be resolved through direct communication.

If unresolved, commission may be withheld until resolved.
