## ‚ö†Ô∏è LEGAL & ETHICAL CONSIDERATIONS

### **What You CAN Do:**
- ‚úÖ Showcase the design/mockup as "concept work"
- ‚úÖ Use it as a portfolio piece
- ‚úÖ Say "Concept design for [Business Type]"
- ‚úÖ Use generic business names if needed
- ‚úÖ Show the design without their actual business name/logo

### **What You SHOULD Do:**
- ‚úÖ Label it as "Concept" or "Design Study"
- ‚úÖ Use generic names if you're concerned (e.g., "Local Restaurant" instead of "Joe's Pizza")
- ‚úÖ Focus on showcasing your design skills, not their business
- ‚úÖ If you use their actual name, make it clear it's a concept/proposal

### **What to AVOID:**
- ‚ùå Don't claim it's a "live site" if it's not
- ‚ùå Don't use their logo/branding without permission (if you're concerned)
- ‚ùå Don't make false claims about results
- ‚ùå Don't use copyrighted images without permission

**Legal Reality:**
- Mockups/concepts are generally fine to showcase
- You created the design, you own the design
- They didn't pay for it, so it's yours
- Just be honest that it's a concept, not a live site
