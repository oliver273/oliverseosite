## üìã STEP-BY-STEP OPERATION

### **Phase 1: Data Collection (Day 1)**

**Step 1: Scrape GMB Data**
- Use your GMB scraper tool
- Target industries:
  - Restaurants
  - Salons/Barbershops
  - Contractors (plumbers, electricians, HVAC)
  - Service businesses
  - Local retail
  - Professional services (lawyers, accountants)

**Step 2: Filter Results**
- Look for businesses with:
  - ‚ùå Bad/outdated websites
  - ‚ùå No website
  - ‚ùå Not mobile-friendly
  - ‚ùå Slow loading
  - ‚ùå Old design (2010s style)

**Step 3: Create Spreadsheet**
```
Columns:
- Business Name
- Website URL
- Phone
- Email (leave blank for now)
- Industry
- Issues Found (bad mobile, outdated, etc.)
- Status (Not Contacted, Emailed, Responded, Mockup Sent, Closed, Lost)
- Notes
- Date Contacted
- Follow-up Date
```

**Step 4: Get Email Addresses**
- For each business, use Hunter.io to find email
- Common formats to try:
  - info@[domain]
  - hello@[domain]
  - contact@[domain]
  - [firstname]@[domain]
  - [firstname].[lastname]@[domain]

**Goal:** Build list of 50-100 businesses ready to contact

**Time:** 2-3 hours

**Email Template 2: Direct Approach**
```

**Email Template 3: Value-First Approach**
```
Subject: I can help [Business Name] get more customers online

Hi [Name],

I saw [Business Name]'s website and noticed [specific observation]. Most businesses with outdated sites are losing customers to competitors with better online presence.

I'm a web designer, and I'd be happy to create a free demo site showing what a modern website could do for your business. No obligation - just want to show you the possibilities.

Interested? Reply and I'll create one for you.

Oliver

**Personalization Tips:**
- Use their actual business name
- Mention specific issue (outdated, not mobile-friendly, etc.)
- Keep it short (3-4 sentences)
- Make it easy to say yes

**Volume:**
- Send 20-30 emails/day
- Space them out (don't send all at once)
- Best times: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (their timezone)

**Update Spreadsheet:**
- Mark "Emailed" status
- Add date contacted
- Set follow-up date (3 days)

**Time:** 1-2 hours/day

## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS

### **Issue 1: Low Response Rate (<5%)**

**Solutions:**
- Improve email personalization
- Test different subject lines
- Try different email templates
- Check email deliverability (see email guide)
- Follow up more (most responses come on follow-up #2-3)

### **Issue 2: High Response Rate But Low Conversion**

**Solutions:**
- Improve mockup quality
- Better offer/pricing
- Faster response time
- Better follow-up sequence
- Address objections better

### **Issue 3: Can't Find Emails**

**Solutions:**
- Try different email formats
- Use LinkedIn to find contacts
- Call business to get email
- Use contact form on their website
- Try social media (Facebook, Instagram)

### **Issue 4: Too Many "No" Responses**

**Solutions:**
- Better targeting (only contact businesses with bad sites)
- Improve value proposition
- Test different offers
- Better timing (when to send emails)

### **Issue 5: Not Enough Time**

**Solutions:**
- Automate what you can (email templates)
- Batch work (do all emails at once)
- Focus on high-value activities
- Consider hiring help (virtual assistant)
