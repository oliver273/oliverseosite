## üìû PHONE SALES AUTOMATION

### **The System:**

**AI Phone Agent:**
- Answers calls 24/7
- Uses your cloned voice (ElevenLabs)
- Sells your services
- Qualifies leads
- Books meetings or closes deals

**Tools:**
- **Vapi.ai** ($50/month) - Conversational AI phone agent
- **Bland AI** ($99/month) - Alternative option
- **ElevenLabs** ($250-350/month) - Voice cloning
- **Twilio** ($39/month) - Phone number

**The Sales Script:**
```
"Hi, this is [Your Name]'s AI assistant speaking in his voice. I'm here to help you get your website started quickly. Is that alright?

[If yes] Great! I help businesses get professional websites. What kind of business are you looking to get a website for?

[Qualify] What's your budget for this project?

[If $2k+] Perfect! I can create a custom 5-page SEO-optimized website for $2,000. Would you like me to send you the details and get started?

[Upsell] Since we're building from scratch, would you like to include our SEO-Booster package and monthly hosting/maintenance for $150/month? It keeps your site fast and secure.

[Downsell if too high] I understand. I can do a 1-page landing page for $900 to get you online professionally. We can always add pages later.

[Close] I'll send the invoice and contract to your email now. Once paid, we start immediately."
```

**Important: Legal Disclosure**
- Must disclose it's AI (legally required in 2026)
- "This is [Your Name]'s AI assistant speaking in his voice"
- Being upfront builds trust (shows you're tech-forward)

**Options:**
- **Option A:** AI closes the deal (sends invoice immediately)
- **Option B:** AI qualifies, then transfers to you or books meeting

**Recommended:** Start with Option B (qualify + book meeting), then move to Option A once it's working well.
