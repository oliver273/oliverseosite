## ðŸ“ˆ OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### **Week 1-2: Testing Phase**

**What to test:**
- Different ad creatives (images, videos, text)
- Different audiences (ages, interests, locations)

### **Week 3-4: Optimization Phase**

**What to do:**
- Turn off ads that don't work

- Improve landing page

**Budget:** $20-30/day
**Goal:** Scale what works

### **Month 2+: Scaling Phase**

**What to do:**
- Double down on winning ads
- Increase budget gradually
- Test new variations
- Expand to new audiences

**Budget:** $30-50/day
**Goal:** Maximize ROI

## âš ï¸ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Spending too much too fast**
   - Start small ($10-20/day)
   - Test first, then scale

2. **No landing page**
   - Don't just link to homepage
   - Create dedicated landing page

3. **Poor targeting**
   - Too broad = waste money
   - Too narrow = no impressions
   - Find the sweet spot

4. **Bad ad creative**
   - Low quality images = low clicks
   - No clear value proposition = no interest
   - Test different creatives

5. **Not tracking results**
   - Don't know what works = can't optimize
   - Track clicks, leads, clients, revenue

6. **Slow response time**
   - Leads go cold fast
   - Reply within 24 hours (ideally same day)

7. **No follow-up**
   - Most sales happen after follow-up
   - Follow up 2-3 times minimum
