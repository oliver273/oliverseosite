## What is Claw Back?

**Claw back** = When you pay a sales rep commission, but then:
- Client cancels before paying
- Client gets a refund
- Client doesn't pay the final 50%
- Deal falls through after commission is paid

**The Problem:** You paid commission, but didn't actually get the money (or lost it).

---

## üéØ THE SOLUTION: Smart Payment Structure

### Option 1: Pay Commission on Final Payment (RECOMMENDED)

**How It Works:**
- Client pays 50% deposit ‚Üí You DON'T pay commission yet
- You build the website
- Client pays final 50% ‚Üí You pay commission NOW

**Why This Works:**
- If client cancels after deposit, you keep the deposit (covers your work)
- Commission only paid when you get FULL payment
- No claw back needed (you never paid commission on incomplete deals)

**Example:**
- Essential package: $997
- Deposit (50%): $498 ‚Üí No commission paid
- Final payment (50%): $499 ‚Üí Pay commission ($399) NOW
- If client cancels after deposit: You keep $498, no commission owed

**Tell Your Sales Rep:**
```
Commission Structure:
- Client pays 50% deposit ‚Üí I start building
- Client pays final 50% ‚Üí I pay you commission within 7 days
- If client cancels after deposit: No commission (they didn't complete payment)
```

---

### Option 2: Pay Commission on Deposit, But with Claw Back Clause

**How It Works:**
- Client pays 50% deposit ‚Üí You pay commission
- If client cancels/refunds ‚Üí You "claw back" the commission

**Why This is Riskier:**
- You pay commission before getting full payment
- If client cancels, you have to get commission back
- Harder to enforce (sales rep might not pay it back)

**When to Use:**
- Only if sales rep insists on deposit payment
- Only with experienced, trusted sales reps
- Only with a written contract

## üîí CONTRACT PROTECTION

### Simple Independent Contractor Agreement

**What to Include:**

1. **Commission Structure:**
   ```
   Commission is paid ONLY when client completes full payment (both deposit and final payment).
   
   Commission rates:
   - First client: 40-50% of total package price
   - Ongoing: 25-30% of total package price
   ```

2. **Payment Timing:**
   ```
   Commission will be paid within 7 days of receiving client's final payment.
   
   Commission is NOT paid on deposits alone.
   ```

3. **Cancellation Policy:**
   ```
   If client cancels after deposit but before final payment:
   - No commission is owed
   - Deposit is retained by [Your Business Name] to cover work completed
   ```

4. **Refund Policy:**
   ```
   If client requests refund within 30 days of final payment:
   - Commission will be clawed back (returned to [Your Business Name])
   - Sales rep must return commission within 14 days
   ```

5. **Dispute Resolution:**
   ```
   Any disputes will be resolved through direct communication.
   If unresolved, commission may be withheld until resolved.
   ```

**Solutions:**

- If no refund requested, pay commission
- If refund requested, don't pay commission

**Solution B: Claw Back Clause in Contract**
- Contract states: "If refund within 30 days, commission must be returned"
- Enforce through contract
- May be hard to enforce if sales rep doesn't cooperate

**Solution C: Accept the Loss (If Small)**
- If refund is rare, accept the loss
- Factor it into your pricing
- Still better than paying commission on deposits

## ‚ö†Ô∏è WHAT IF SALES REP WON'T RETURN COMMISSION?

### If Claw Back is Needed But Sales Rep Refuses:

**Options:**

1. **Deduct from Future Commissions:**
   - If they close another sale, deduct the claw back amount
   - Example: They owe $399, next sale commission is $500 ‚Üí Pay them $101

2. **Terminate Relationship:**
   - If they won't cooperate, end the relationship
   - Don't work with them again
   - Accept the loss (if small)

3. **Legal Action (Last Resort):**
   - Only if large amount ($1000+)
   - Only if you have written contract
   - Usually not worth it for small amounts

**Best Prevention:** Pay commission on final payment only (avoids this problem)
