## üö´ WHAT NOT TO DO

### ‚ùå Don't Offer Free:
- "I'll build you a free website"
- "First client gets it free"
- "I'll do it for exposure"

**Why:** Devalues your work, sets bad precedent, attracts wrong clients

### ‚ùå Don't Be Too Salesy:
- "You NEED a website NOW!"
- "Your business will fail without one"
- "Limited time offer!"

**Why:** Pushes people away, sounds scammy

### ‚ùå Don't Be Vague:
- "I can help with websites"
- "Want a website?"
- "I do websites"

**Why:** Doesn't show value, doesn't differentiate you

---

## ‚úÖ WHAT TO DO INSTEAD

### ‚úÖ Offer Value First:
- Free audit
- Free consultation
- Helpful advice

### ‚úÖ Be Specific:
- "I help Wyoming businesses get 2-3x more customers"
- "I build websites that actually get phone calls"
- "I've helped 3 Wyoming businesses already"

### ‚úÖ Show Understanding:
- "I know small businesses struggle with..."
- "I understand budget is important..."
- "I get that you're busy..."
