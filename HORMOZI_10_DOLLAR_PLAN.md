## Hormozi's Core Principle

**"Do things that don't scale to learn what works, then scale what works."**

With $10, you can't run ads. But you CAN:
1. Give away free value to get leads
2. Build proof (testimonials, portfolio)
3. Create an upsell engine
4. THEN run ads once you have cash flow

## THE FREE WEBSITE GIVEAWAY FUNNEL

### The Concept

**You offer:** 1 FREE website/week (or month)
**They give you:** Email, testimonial, and referrals
**You upsell:** Domain, hosting, maintenance, additional pages

### Why This Works (Hormozi Logic)

1. **Lead Magnet** - Free website attracts TONS of leads
2. **Proof** - You get testimonials and portfolio pieces
3. **Upsells** - They need domain/hosting (recurring revenue!)
4. **Referrals** - Happy clients tell friends
5. **Email List** - Everyone who enters becomes a warm lead

### Step 1: Create the Giveaway Landing Page

**Selection criteria:**

- Someone who will give a great testimonial

**Announce winner:**

- Email everyone who entered
- Creates FOMO for next giveaway

   - Announce the winner
   - Show excitement

### Phase 2: Run Ads (After First $500-1000)

**Don't run ads until:**
- You have testimonials

- You have cash to invest

2. Target lookalikes of your customers
3. Start with $20-50/day

## THE EMAIL LIST STRATEGY

### Building the List

**Everyone who enters your giveaway = email subscriber**

**Goal:** 100-500 emails in first month

### Nurturing the List

**Weekly email sequence:**

**Email 1 (Day 0):** Thanks for entering! Here's what to expect.
**Email 2 (Day 3):** Tips for your business website (value)
**Email 3 (Day 7):** Winner announcement + consolation offer
**Email 4 (Day 14):** Case study from recent client
**Email 5 (Day 21):** Special offer (limited time)

### Converting the List

**After giveaway ends:**
- Everyone gets offer emails
- Limited time discounts
- New giveaway announcements
- Valuable content

**Expected list value:** $1-5 per subscriber per month
