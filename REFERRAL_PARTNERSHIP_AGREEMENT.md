## Simple Referral Partnership Agreement

**Between:** OliverSEO (Oliver)  
**And:** [Partner Company Name - e.g., Vishionary]

---

## Agreement Overview

This is a simple, flexible referral partnership where [Partner] refers clients who need website services to OliverSEO, and receives a commission for successful referrals.

---

## Terms

### 1. Referral Process
- [Partner] identifies clients who need website design/development services
- [Partner] introduces client to OliverSEO (via email, phone, or in-person)
- OliverSEO handles all client communication, proposals, and project delivery
- [Partner] receives commission upon project completion and payment

### 2. Commission Structure
- **Commission Rate:** [15-20%] of total project value
- **Payment Terms:** Commission paid within 7 days of receiving full payment from client
- **Payment Method:** [Check, PayPal, Bank Transfer, etc.]

### 3. Responsibilities

**OliverSEO Responsibilities:**
- Provide professional website design/development services
- Handle all client communication and project management
- Deliver quality work on time
- Provide client support and revisions as agreed
- Pay referral commission promptly

**Partner Responsibilities:**
- Make genuine referrals (clients who actually need websites)
- Provide basic introduction to OliverSEO
- Not make false promises about services or pricing

### 4. Client Relationship
- Once referred, client becomes OliverSEO's client
- OliverSEO is responsible for all client communication and service delivery
- Partner is not responsible for project outcomes or client satisfaction
- Partner may check in with client, but OliverSEO handles all project details

### 5. Pricing & Proposals
- OliverSEO sets all pricing and creates all proposals
- Partner may provide input on client budget/needs
- Final pricing decisions rest with OliverSEO

### 6. Exclusivity
- [ ] Non-exclusive: Partner can refer to other web designers
- [ ] Exclusive: Partner agrees to refer only to OliverSEO (optional)

### 7. Term & Termination
- **Term:** This agreement continues until either party terminates
- **Termination:** Either party can terminate with 30 days written notice
- **Ongoing Projects:** Projects in progress at termination will be completed, commissions paid

### 8. Confidentiality
- Both parties agree to keep client information confidential
- Partner will not share OliverSEO's pricing/processes with competitors
- OliverSEO will not contact Partner's clients without referral
