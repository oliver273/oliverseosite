A sales funnel guides potential clients from first contact to paying customer through strategic steps.

---

## STAGE 1: AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)

**Goal:** Get people to know you exist

### Actions:
1. **Post on Platforms**
   - Fiverr (create gig)
   - Upwork (apply to jobs)
   - Reddit (post in r/forhire)
   - LinkedIn (share content)

2. **Share on Social Media**
   - LinkedIn posts
   - Twitter/X posts
   - Facebook posts
   - Instagram (if applicable)

3. **Answer Questions**
   - Quora (answer questions)
   - Reddit (help in forums)
   - Facebook groups (be helpful)
   - LinkedIn comments

4. **Create Content**
   - Blog posts
   - Free guides
   - Video tutorials
   - Case studies

### Tools Needed:
- Social media accounts
- Portfolio website
- Content (blog posts, guides)
- Email list (start building)

### Metrics to Track:
- Website visitors
- Social media followers
- Content views
- Email subscribers

---

## STAGE 2: INTEREST (Middle of Funnel)

**Goal:** Get people interested in your service

### Actions:
1. **Offer Free Value**
   - Free guide download
   - Free checklist
   - Free consultation
   - Free resources

2. **Show Portfolio**
   - Examples of your work
   - Case studies
   - Before/after examples
   - Client testimonials

3. **Share Testimonials**
   - Client reviews
   - Success stories
   - Social proof
   - Ratings

4. **Provide Helpful Content**
   - Answer questions
   - Share tips
   - Provide value
   - Build trust

### Tools Needed:
- Landing page with lead magnet
- Email capture form
- Portfolio page
- Testimonials page
- Email marketing tool (Mailchimp, ConvertKit)

### Metrics to Track:
- Email signups
- Guide downloads
- Consultation bookings
- Portfolio views

---

## STAGE 3: CONSIDERATION (Bottom of Funnel)

**Goal:** Get people to consider buying

### Actions:
1. **Send Proposal**
   - Detailed, personalized
   - Address their needs
   - Clear pricing
   - Timeline

2. **Offer Consultation**
   - Free 15-minute call
   - Understand their needs
   - Build rapport
   - Answer questions

3. **Show Case Studies**
   - Real results
   - Before/after
   - Client success stories
   - ROI examples

4. **Address Objections**
   - FAQ page
   - Guarantee
   - Payment plans
   - Risk reversal

### Tools Needed:
- Proposal template
- Consultation booking system (Calendly)
- Case studies
- FAQ page
- Contract template

### Metrics to Track:
- Proposals sent
- Consultations booked
- Questions answered
- Objections handled

---

## STAGE 4: PURCHASE (Conversion)

**Goal:** Get people to buy

### Actions:
1. **Send Invoice**
   - Square invoice
   - Clear pricing
   - Payment terms
   - Due date

2. **Collect Payment**
   - 50% deposit
   - Secure payment
   - Receipt sent
   - Confirmation

3. **Start Project**
   - Kickoff call
   - Project brief
   - Timeline agreed
   - Communication plan

4. **Deliver Results**
   - On time
   - Quality work
   - Exceed expectations
   - Follow up

### Tools Needed:
- Square account (invoicing)
- Contract template
- Project management (Trello, Asana)
- Communication tools (email, Slack)
- File sharing (Google Drive, Dropbox)

### Metrics to Track:
- Invoices sent
- Payments received
- Projects started
- Delivery time

---

## STAGE 5: RETENTION (After Purchase)

**Goal:** Keep clients happy and get referrals

### Actions:
1. **Deliver Quality Work**
   - Exceed expectations
   - On time delivery
   - Professional quality
   - Clear communication

2. **Follow Up**
   - Check in after delivery
   - Ask for feedback
   - Offer support
   - Stay in touch

3. **Ask for Reviews**
   - Testimonials
   - Reviews on platforms
   - Case studies
   - Referrals

4. **Offer Additional Services**
   - Website updates
   - SEO services
   - Maintenance plans
   - New features

### Tools Needed:
- Follow-up email template
- Review request template
- Upsell offers
- Email marketing (nurture sequence)

### Metrics to Track:
- Client satisfaction
- Reviews received
- Referrals
- Repeat business
