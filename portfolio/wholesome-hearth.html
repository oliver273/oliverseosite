Our story

Baked slowly, shared warmly.

Wholesome Hearth began as a single sourdough starter and a tiny home oven. What started as loaves for friends and neighbors has grown into a cozy corner bakery where everything is mixed, folded, and baked by hand.

We mill a portion of our grains in-house, use long fermentation for better flavor and digestion, and source eggs, dairy, and produce from nearby organic farms. Our shelves change with the seasons, but our promise stays the same: bread that nourishes, pastries that comfort, and a space that feels like home.
