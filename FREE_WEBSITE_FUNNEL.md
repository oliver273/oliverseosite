### When They Apply, Offer:

## Quick Start: Free vs Paid Ads

### Option A: Free Content First (No Budget)
1. Post videos on TikTok, Reels, YouTube
2. "Free website giveaway" in video
3. Link to application form
4. Convert organically

**Pros:** No cost, builds audience
**Cons:** Slower, less predictable

### Option B: Paid Ads ($500-1,000/month)
1. Run Facebook/Instagram ads
2. Target small business owners, entrepreneurs
3. Send to landing page
4. Convert faster

**Pros:** Faster, scalable, predictable
**Cons:** Costs money, need to learn ads

### Recommended: Start Free, Add Paid Later
1. **Month 1:** Free content, prove the offer works
2. **Month 2:** If getting applications, start with $10-20/day ads
3. **Month 3+:** Scale what works
