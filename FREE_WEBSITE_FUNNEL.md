# Free Website Giveaway Funnel

## The Strategy

**Give away 1 free website** ‚Üí **Build email list** ‚Üí **Upsell domain/hosting** ‚Üí **Convert to paid clients**

This is a lead magnet funnel that builds trust and gets you paying customers.

---

## How It Works

```
VIDEO ADS ‚Üí LANDING PAGE ‚Üí FREE GIVEAWAY ‚Üí EMAIL LIST ‚Üí UPSELLS ‚Üí PAID CLIENTS
```

### Step 1: Create Video Ads
- Record yourself building a website
- Show the value of what you do
- Drive traffic to your landing page

### Step 2: Landing Page
- "Enter to win a FREE $2,500 website"
- Collect emails
- Build your list

### Step 3: Pick a Winner
- Give away 1 free website
- EVERYONE else gets an offer
- "You didn't win, but here's 50% off"

### Step 4: Upsell Winners
- Free website, BUT...
- Domain: $15/year
- Hosting: $10-20/month
- Maintenance: $50-100/month

### Step 5: Convert Losers
- "You didn't win, but..."
- Special offer for entrants
- They're already warm leads

---

## The Math

### Giveaway Math:
- **Cost to you:** 5-10 hours building 1 free site
- **What you get:** 100-500 email leads
- **Conversion rate:** 2-5% become paying clients
- **Result:** 2-25 clients from 1 giveaway

### Example:
- 200 people enter giveaway
- 1 person wins (free site + upsells)
- 199 people get "consolation offer" (30-50% off)
- 10 people (5%) take the offer
- **10 clients √ó $1,500 = $15,000**
