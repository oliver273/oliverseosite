## üõ°Ô∏è PROTECTING YOURSELF

### "Unlimited Revisions" - How to Not Get Abused

**What you SAY:** "Unlimited revisions until you're happy"
**What you MEAN:** Tweaks to make it right, not endless rebuilds

**Set expectations early:**
```
Just so you know - revisions means tweaking what we build 
(colors, layout, text, images). If you want to add new pages 
or features later, I'll quote those separately. Fair?
```

**If they abuse it (rare):**
```
Hey, we're on revision #6 now and the requests are getting 
into new territory (adding features we didn't discuss).

Happy to keep going, but this would be an add-on at $150.
Or we can launch what we have and add features later.

What works for you?
```

### Money-Back Guarantee - How to Not Lose Money

**The rule:** Refund available ONLY before launch

**How to say it:**
```
My guarantee: if you don't love the design before we launch, 
full refund. But once you approve and we go live, we're done.

That way you're protected, and I'm protected. Fair?
```

**Why this works:**
- They feel safe trying you (more sales)
- Once they see the preview, they almost never want a refund
- If they DO want a refund, you only lost time, not a launched site
- Launched = proof of work = no chargebacks

### The Numbers

| Scenario | How Often | Your Risk |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Client loves it, pays in full | 90% | None |
| Client wants tweaks, then loves it | 8% | A few hours |
| Client wants refund before launch | 2% | Lost time, kept reputation |
| Client wants refund AFTER launch | <1% | Contract protects you |

**The guarantee MAKES you more money than it costs.**
Without it: fewer people say yes.
With it: more people say yes, almost none ask for refund.
