   - Save time

   - Invoice reminders
   - Email sequences
   - Social media scheduling

4. **Set Boundaries**
   - Working hours
   - Response times
   - Project scope
   - Communication channels

5. **Stay Organized**
   - Use project management
   - Keep files organized
   - Track everything
   - Review regularly
