## 3. SQUARE INVOICE SETUP GUIDE

### Step 1: Create Square Account
1. Go to: https://squareup.com
2. Click "Sign Up" (top right)
3. Choose "Invoicing" or "Point of Sale"
4. Enter your email and create password
5. Verify your email

### Step 2: Set Up Your Business Profile
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Business Information
2. Add:
   - Business name
   - Business address
   - Phone number
   - Website (if you have one)
   - Logo (upload your logo)
   - Tax ID (if applicable)

### Step 3: Set Up Payment Methods
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Payment Methods
2. Connect bank account:
   - Click "Add Bank Account"
   - Enter routing and account number
   - Verify with test deposits (takes 1-2 days)
3. Set up credit card processing:
   - Square automatically enables this
   - Fees: 2.6% + $0.10 per transaction

### Step 4: Create Your First Invoice
1. Go to Invoices ‚Üí Create Invoice
2. Fill in:
   - **Customer Info:**
     - Name
     - Email
     - Address (optional)
   - **Invoice Details:**
     - Invoice number (auto-generated or custom)
     - Date
     - Due date
   - **Items:**
     - Description: "Website Design - Professional Package"
     - Quantity: 1
     - Price: $499
     - Tax: Add if applicable
   - **Notes:**
     - Payment terms
     - What's included
     - Delivery timeline

### Step 5: Customize Invoice Template
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Invoice Settings
2. Customize:
   - Logo (upload)
   - Colors (match your brand)
   - Terms and conditions
   - Footer message
   - Payment methods accepted

### Step 6: Send Invoice
1. Click "Send Invoice"
2. Choose method:
   - Email (recommended)
   - Text message
   - Link (copy and send)
3. Client receives email with:
   - Invoice PDF
   - Pay Now button
   - Payment options

### Step 7: Track Payments
1. Go to Invoices ‚Üí View All
2. See status:
   - Sent (waiting for payment)
   - Paid (green checkmark)
   - Overdue (red)
3. Set up notifications:
   - Email when paid
   - Reminder for overdue

### Step 8: Set Up Recurring Invoices (If Needed)
1. Go to Invoices ‚Üí Recurring
2. Create template:
   - Frequency (monthly, etc.)
   - Amount
   - Customer
3. Square sends automatically

### üí° PRO TIPS:
- **Auto-reminders:** Set up automatic reminders for overdue invoices
- **Deposits:** Create separate invoice for 50% deposit
- **Milestones:** Create invoices for project milestones
- **Receipts:** Square automatically sends receipts
- **Reports:** View sales reports in dashboard

### üìä Square Fees:
- **Online payments:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **In-person:** 2.6% + $0.10 per transaction
- **Invoicing:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **No monthly fees** (pay per transaction)
